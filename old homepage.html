About Belle Maison

Gina Schorr

With over 20 years of interior design experience, Belle Maison brings a keen awareness of what it takes to create timeless designs using texture, color, lighting, textiles, and interior finishes. We have the passion and ability to transform your home into a welcoming and comfortable environment.

 

Considering form and function, we will collaborate with you to create a space that represents you and your lifestyle. We believe your home should reflect you, not your designer.

 

Your home environment influences every aspect of your life and should always be a place that surrounds you with beauty, comfort and calm. Your home really is your castle!

Our Process

We don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all solution. Your needs and tastes are unique. So the process we follow to make your home the functional and beautiful home you have in mind is determined after a face-to-face meeting.

 

We’ll ask questions, discuss ideas, brain storm, discuss a budget that works for you, explore all possibilities. We’ll determine what your starting point is and begin a process that will ultimately end with your design goals becoming a reality.
